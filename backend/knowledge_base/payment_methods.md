# NexaCart Payment Methods

## Rules
- Accepted payment methods: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Shop Pay, and NexaCart Gift Cards
- Buy Now Pay Later (BNPL) is available via Klarna and Afterpay (split into 4 interest-free payments)
- All transactions are processed in USD
- Payments are encrypted using 256-bit SSL encryption
- NexaCart does not store full credit card numbers on its servers
- Gift cards can be used in combination with other payment methods
- Multiple gift cards can be applied to a single order
- NexaCart Store Credit is applied automatically before other payment methods
- Promo codes and coupons must be applied at checkout and cannot be applied retroactively
- Currency conversion fees for international cards are charged by the card issuer, not NexaCart

## Edge Cases
- If a payment fails, the customer should verify card details, check with their bank, or try a different payment method
- Duplicate charges: if a customer is charged twice for one order, they should contact support with the order number and both charge amounts
- Klarna/Afterpay availability depends on order total (minimum $35, maximum $1,500)
- If a gift card balance is insufficient to cover the full order, the remaining balance must be paid with another method
- Refunds for orders paid with gift cards are returned to the gift card, not to cash
- Refunds to credit/debit cards may take 5–10 business days depending on the bank
- PayPal disputes should be opened through NexaCart support first before escalating to PayPal
- If a promo code doesn't work at checkout, it may be expired, already used, or not applicable to the items in cart

## Frequently Asked Questions
- Q: What payment methods do you accept? A: We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Shop Pay, NexaCart Gift Cards, Klarna, and Afterpay.
- Q: Do you accept PayPal? A: Yes, PayPal is accepted at checkout.
- Q: Can I pay with Apple Pay? A: Yes, Apple Pay and Google Pay are both supported.
- Q: Can I pay with Google Pay? A: Yes, Google Pay is accepted at checkout.
- Q: Can I use Klarna? A: Yes, Klarna is available for orders between $35–$1,500, split into 4 interest-free payments.
- Q: Can I use Afterpay? A: Yes, Afterpay is available for orders between $35–$1,500, split into 4 interest-free payments.
- Q: What is Buy Now Pay Later? A: BNPL lets you split your order into 4 interest-free payments via Klarna or Afterpay. Available for orders $35–$1,500.
- Q: My payment failed. What should I do? A: Check your card details, contact your bank, or use a different payment method.
- Q: Can I use multiple gift cards? A: Yes, you can apply multiple gift cards to one order.
- Q: My promo code isn't working. Why? A: It may be expired, already used, or not valid for items in your cart. Contact support to verify.
- Q: I was charged twice. What should I do? A: Contact support immediately with your order number and both charge amounts.
- Q: Is my payment information secure? A: Yes, all payments use 256-bit SSL encryption and we never store full card numbers.
- Q: Can I apply a promo code after placing my order? A: No, promo codes must be applied at checkout and cannot be added retroactively.
- Q: My gift card balance isn't enough to cover my order. What do I do? A: You can pay the remaining balance with another payment method such as a credit card or PayPal.
- Q: I paid with a gift card and returned an item. Where is my refund? A: Refunds for gift card payments are returned to the original gift card balance.
- Q: Do you accept cryptocurrency? A: No, NexaCart does not currently accept cryptocurrency payments.
- Q: Can I split payment between a card and a gift card? A: Yes, gift cards can be combined with any other payment method.
