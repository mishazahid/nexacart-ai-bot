# NexaCart Return Policy

## Rules
- Customers have 30 days from delivery date to initiate a return
- Items must be unused, unwashed, and in original packaging with all tags attached
- Proof of purchase (order number or receipt) is required for all returns
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5–7 business days after the returned item is received and inspected
- Return shipping is free for defective or incorrect items
- For change-of-mind returns, customer pays return shipping ($5.99 label provided by NexaCart)
- Non-returnable items: perishable goods, digital downloads, gift cards, customized/personalized items, intimate apparel, and hazardous materials
- Items marked "Final Sale" cannot be returned or exchanged
- Partial refunds may be issued for items that are returned damaged or missing components
- Returns must be initiated through the account dashboard or by contacting support@nexacart.com

## Edge Cases
- If the 30-day return window falls on a weekend or holiday, it extends to the next business day
- Items purchased during flash sales are still eligible for return unless marked "Final Sale"
- If a customer returns more than 5 orders in a 6-month period, their account may be flagged for review
- Bundle purchases: if one item in a bundle is returned, the discount applied to the bundle may be recalculated
- International returns are accepted but the customer is responsible for all return shipping costs and customs fees
- If a refund is not received within 7 business days, customers should check with their bank before contacting support
- Store credit option: customers can choose store credit instead of a refund and receive an extra 10% bonus value
- Refunds for orders paid with gift cards are returned to the original gift card balance

## Frequently Asked Questions
- Q: How do I return an item? A: Initiate your return within 30 days via your account dashboard or contact support. We'll send a return label.
- Q: Can I return a digital download? A: No, digital downloads are non-returnable.
- Q: How long does my refund take? A: 5–7 business days after we receive and inspect your return.
- Q: Do I pay for return shipping? A: Only for change-of-mind returns ($5.99). Defective or wrong items have free return shipping.
- Q: Can I get store credit instead of a refund? A: Yes! You'll receive 10% bonus value on store credit.
- Q: I missed the 30-day window by one day. Can I still return? A: Contact support — if the deadline fell on a weekend, it extends to the next business day.
- Q: What items cannot be returned? A: Digital downloads, gift cards, perishables, customized items, intimate apparel, hazardous materials, and "Final Sale" items cannot be returned.
- Q: How do I start a return? A: Log into your account dashboard, go to My Orders, select the item, and click Return Item.
- Q: What is the return window? A: You have 30 days from the delivery date to initiate a return.
- Q: I received a defective item. Is return shipping free? A: Yes, return shipping is free for defective or incorrect items sent by NexaCart.
- Q: Can I return a flash sale item? A: Yes, flash sale items can be returned unless they are marked "Final Sale."
- Q: When will I get my money back? A: Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after we receive and inspect your return.
- Q: Can I return without original packaging? A: Items must be in original packaging with tags attached. Contact support if you have concerns.
- Q: I haven't received my refund. What do I do? A: First check with your bank. If it's been more than 7 business days, contact support@nexacart.com.
- Q: Is there a restocking fee? A: No, NexaCart does not charge restocking fees.
