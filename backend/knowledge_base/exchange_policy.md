# NexaCart Exchange Policy

## Rules
- Exchanges are accepted within 30 days of delivery
- Items must be in original, unused condition with tags and packaging intact
- Exchanges are available for different size, color, or variant of the same product
- Exchange shipping is free (both return and re-shipment) for the first exchange per order
- A second exchange on the same order incurs a $5.99 re-shipment fee
- Exchange requests must be submitted through the account dashboard or by contacting support
- If the desired exchange item is out of stock, the customer can: wait for restock, choose a different variant, or receive a full refund
- Price differences: if the new item costs more, the customer pays the difference. If it costs less, the difference is refunded.
- Exchanges are processed within 2–3 business days of receiving the returned item
- Gift exchanges (items received as gifts) require the gift receipt or order number

## Edge Cases
- If an item goes on sale after the exchange is initiated, the sale price does not apply to the exchange
- Exchanges cannot be used to circumvent the return policy (e.g., exchanging a "Final Sale" item)
- International exchanges are not supported — international customers must return the item and place a new order
- If the wrong item was sent by NexaCart, the exchange is processed on priority (1 business day) with no fees
- Customized items cannot be exchanged unless defective
- If a customer requests multiple exchanges simultaneously, only one will be processed at a time
- Size exchanges for footwear require the original box to be returned

## Frequently Asked Questions
- Q: Can I exchange for a different size? A: Yes, within 30 days in original condition. Submit via your dashboard.
- Q: Can I exchange for a different color? A: Yes, exchanges are available for different size, color, or variant of the same product.
- Q: Is exchange shipping free? A: Yes, for the first exchange per order. A second exchange has a $5.99 re-shipment fee.
- Q: What if my size is out of stock? A: You can wait for restock, pick another variant, or get a full refund.
- Q: I received the wrong item. How fast will my exchange be? A: Priority processing — 1 business day, no fees.
- Q: Can I exchange an international order? A: No, international customers must return and re-order.
- Q: The new item I want costs more. What happens? A: You'll pay the price difference at the time of exchange.
- Q: The new item costs less than what I paid. Do I get money back? A: Yes, the price difference will be refunded to your original payment method.
- Q: How long does an exchange take? A: 2–3 business days after we receive your returned item.
- Q: Can I exchange a gift? A: Yes, with a gift receipt or the original order number.
- Q: Can I do a second exchange? A: Yes, but a second exchange on the same order has a $5.99 re-shipment fee.
- Q: What is the exchange window? A: You have 30 days from the delivery date to request an exchange.
- Q: Can I exchange a customized item? A: Only if it is defective. Customized items cannot be exchanged for size or color.
- Q: How do I start an exchange? A: Log into your account dashboard, go to My Orders, select the item, and click Exchange Item.
- Q: Can I exchange shoes? A: Yes, but footwear exchanges require the original shoe box to be returned.
