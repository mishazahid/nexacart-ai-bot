# NexaCart Shipping Policy

## Rules
- Standard shipping: 3–5 business days, $4.99 flat fee
- Express shipping: 1–2 business days, $12.99 flat fee
- Free standard shipping on all orders over $50
- International shipping: 7–14 business days, $24.99 flat fee (available to 30+ countries)
- Orders placed before 2 PM EST are processed same day
- Orders placed after 2 PM EST or on weekends are processed next business day
- Shipping is available to all 50 US states including Alaska and Hawaii
- PO Boxes are supported for standard shipping only
- No shipping to freight forwarders
- Carriers used: UPS, FedEx, USPS depending on destination and order size
- Tracking number is emailed once order ships, usually within 24 hours of processing

## Edge Cases
- If an order contains both in-stock and pre-order items, the entire order ships when all items are available unless the customer requests split shipping
- During peak seasons (Black Friday, Christmas), delivery times may increase by 2–3 business days
- Hawaii and Alaska standard shipping takes 5–7 business days (not 3–5)
- If a package is marked delivered but not received, customers must wait 24 hours before filing a lost package claim
- Shipping fees are non-refundable unless NexaCart made an error
- Weather delays and carrier disruptions are outside NexaCart's control and do not qualify for refunds
- Customers can add shipping insurance for $1.99 per order (covers up to $200 in losses)
- For orders over $200, signature confirmation is automatically added at no charge
- Split shipping is available on request for mixed in-stock and pre-order carts
- APO/FPO military addresses are supported via USPS standard shipping only

- Q: How long does standard shipping take? A: Standard shipping takes 3–5 business days.
- Q: How long does express shipping take? A: Express shipping takes 1–2 business days.
- Q: How much does shipping cost? A: Standard shipping is $4.99. Express shipping is $12.99. Free standard shipping on orders over $50.
- Q: Is shipping free? A: Yes, standard shipping is free on orders over $50.
- Q: Can I ship to a PO Box? A: Yes, PO Boxes are supported for standard shipping only.
- Q: Do you ship internationally? A: Yes, to 30+ countries. International delivery takes 7–14 business days for $24.99.
- Q: How long will my order take? A: Standard orders take 3–5 business days. Express orders arrive in 1–2 business days.
- Q: My package says delivered but I don't have it. A: Please wait 24 hours and check with neighbors. If still missing, contact support.
- Q: What happens if I order a mix of in-stock and pre-order items? A: Your entire order ships when all items are ready, unless you request split shipping.
- Q: Can I track my order? A: Yes, a tracking number is emailed to you once your order ships.
- Q: What carriers do you use? A: We use UPS, FedEx, and USPS depending on your location and order size.
- Q: Do you ship to Alaska and Hawaii? A: Yes, standard shipping to Alaska and Hawaii takes 5–7 business days.
- Q: Do you ship to APO/FPO addresses? A: Yes, via USPS standard shipping.
- Q: Can I add shipping insurance? A: Yes, for $1.99 per order it covers losses up to $200.
- Q: What time do I need to order for same-day processing? A: Place your order before 2 PM EST for same-day processing.
- Q: How do I get free shipping? A: Orders over $50 automatically qualify for free standard shipping.
- Q: Can I change my shipping address after ordering? A: Contact support immediately — address changes are only possible before the order ships.
- Q: My tracking number isn't working. A: Tracking may take up to 24 hours to activate after the shipping confirmation email is sent.
