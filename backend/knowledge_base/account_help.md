# NexaCart Account Help

## Rules
- Customers can create a free NexaCart account at nexacart.com/register
- Password must be at least 8 characters with one uppercase letter, one number, and one special character
- Password reset is available via the "Forgot Password" link on the login page — a reset link is sent to the registered email
- Email address can be changed in Account Settings > Personal Information (verification required for the new email)
- Order history is available in the Account Dashboard under "My Orders" for the past 2 years
- Customers can save multiple shipping addresses in their address book
- Two-factor authentication (2FA) can be enabled in Account Settings > Security using an authenticator app or SMS
- Account deletion is permanent and removes all personal data within 30 days per GDPR/CCPA compliance
- Deleted accounts cannot be restored; all order history and rewards points are permanently lost
- Customers can download a copy of their personal data from Account Settings > Privacy > Download My Data

## Edge Cases
- If a customer can't access their registered email for password reset, they must contact support with identity verification (order number + last 4 digits of payment method used)
- Accounts inactive for more than 2 years may be deactivated; customer data is retained for legal compliance
- If 2FA codes are not received via SMS, the customer should check spam, verify their phone number, or use an authenticator app instead
- Multiple accounts with the same email are not allowed; if a customer tries to register with an existing email, they'll be prompted to log in or reset their password
- Rewards points are non-transferable between accounts
- Business accounts have separate registration at nexacart.com/business and offer bulk pricing
- If a customer places an order as a guest and later creates an account, guest orders cannot be retroactively linked to the account

## Frequently Asked Questions
- Q: How do I reset my password? A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your email. You'll receive a reset link within 5 minutes.
- Q: How do I enable 2FA? A: Go to Account Settings > Security > Two-Factor Authentication and follow the setup steps.
- Q: How do I enable two-factor authentication? A: Go to Account Settings > Security and enable Two-Factor Authentication using an authenticator app or SMS.
- Q: Can I delete my account? A: Yes, but deletion is permanent. Go to Account Settings > Privacy > Delete Account.
- Q: I can't access my email for password reset. What do I do? A: Contact support with your order number and the last 4 digits of your payment method for identity verification.
- Q: Where can I see my past orders? A: In your Account Dashboard under "My Orders" (up to 2 years of history).
- Q: Can I have two accounts with the same email? A: No, each email can only be associated with one NexaCart account.
- Q: How do I change my email address? A: Go to Account Settings > Personal Information and update your email. Verification will be sent to the new address.
- Q: How do I change my password? A: Go to Account Settings > Security > Change Password, or use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.
- Q: How do I create an account? A: Visit nexacart.com/register and fill in your name, email, and a strong password.
- Q: I forgot my password. What do I do? A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and follow the reset link sent to your inbox.
- Q: How do I download my personal data? A: Go to Account Settings > Privacy > Download My Data.
- Q: My 2FA code is not arriving. What should I do? A: Check your spam folder, verify your phone number is correct, or switch to an authenticator app.
- Q: Can I save multiple shipping addresses? A: Yes, you can save multiple addresses in your account address book.
- Q: What are the password requirements? A: At least 8 characters with one uppercase letter, one number, and one special character.
- Q: I placed an order as a guest. Can I link it to my account? A: No, guest orders cannot be retroactively linked to an account.
- Q: Can I use a business account? A: Yes, register at nexacart.com/business for bulk pricing and business features.
- Q: How do I log in? A: Go to nexacart.com/login and enter your email and password.
